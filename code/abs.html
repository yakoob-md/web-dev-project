Hand Planks

Beginner

Step 1: Start in a kneeling position with your hands planted on the ground.
Step 2:Pick your knees up off the ground. Hold in this position with a flat back.

Barbell Situp

Intermediate

Step 1:Hold the bar with a slightly wider than shoulder width grip. Push the bar up toward the ceiling and maintain that elbow extension.
Step 2:Flex your abs and sit-up. Aim to get your torso vertical while maintaining the barbell overhead.

Dumbbell Twist

Intermediate

Step 1:Sit on the floor and flex your knees and hips to a 90 degree angle.
Step 2: Your feet should be hovering off the ground. (If that's too hard start with heels on the floor)
Step 3:Rotate your upper spine to engage your obliques

Laying Leg Raises

Beginner

Step 1:Lay on your back with your arms palms down on either side.
Step 2:Keep your legs together and as straight as possible.
Step 3:Slowly raise your legs to a 90Â° angle, pause at this position, or as high as you can reach your legs, and then slowly lower your legs back down.
